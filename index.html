About

Rohit R Chauhan is an entrepreneur and financial innovator known for building modern investing solutions designed around real-life financial behavior. As the Founder of InGood, he focuses on creating systems that make investing more flexible, practical, and less stressful for everyday users.

His work is driven by the belief that financial products should adapt to people’s lifestyles instead of forcing people to adapt to rigid financial structures.

Before creating InGood, Rohit R Chauhan closely observed how many individuals struggled to maintain consistent investments despite having the intention to build wealth.

He identified that unpredictable monthly expenses, irregular income patterns, and fear of losing liquidity were some of the biggest reasons why people stopped investing.

This understanding inspired him to rethink traditional SIP models and develop a smarter, more adaptive investing approach.

The Future of Investing

Through the InGood App, Rohit introduced the concept of the Self-Adjusting SIP, a system that automatically adapts investment amounts based on a user’s real cash flow and spending behavior.

Powered by InGoodfinserv, the platform focuses on cash flow–based investing, intelligent automation, liquidity-first planning, and financial behavior insights to help users invest consistently without financial pressure.

Today, Rohit R Chauhan continues to share ideas around behavioral finance, investing psychology, and adaptive wealth creation.

His vision is to make investing simpler, more sustainable, and more accessible for modern investors by building systems that support long-term financial growth while respecting the realities of everyday financial life.
